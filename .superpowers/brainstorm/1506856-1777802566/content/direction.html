What's the core design goal?

This shapes every decision across all 6 areas. Pick the direction that feels right.

A

Maximum Game Feel

Make the game more visceral and satisfying. Bigger hit effects, screen shake, kill streaks, combo feedback, death explosions, muzzle flashes. The UI should feel like a cockpit that pulses with the action.


Think: Vampire Survivors × Hades — juicy, arcade, addictive.

B

Clarity & Information

Make the game easier to read at a glance. Better stat displays, clearer upgrade comparisons, tooltip system, improved boss/objective alerts, more readable HUD under chaos.


Think: Path of Exile × Dead Cells — informed decisions, clear progression.

C

Visual Polish & Cohesion

Elevate the visual quality across all screens. Better animations, consistent design language, cinematic moments (boss spawn zoom, level-up fanfare), premium "shipped game" feel.


Think: Balatro × Slay the Spire — distinctive, beautiful, memorable.

D

All of the above — balanced

A bit of each: more juice in combat, clearer information in menus/upgrades, and a cohesive visual upgrade across all screens. Prioritise the low-hanging fruit first.


Think: a full "version 2.0" visual update.
