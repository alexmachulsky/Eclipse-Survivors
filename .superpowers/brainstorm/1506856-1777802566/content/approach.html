How do you want to roll this out?

This shapes the implementation plan structure.

A

Impact-first across all areas

Pick the highest-bang-for-buck change in each of the 6 areas and ship them together. Broad coverage, focused effort — avoids rabbit holes. Best for "make it feel noticeably better fast."


~1 week of focused work. Noticeable improvement everywhere.

B

One area at a time, fully polished

Go deep on each area before moving to the next. Start with the most visible (HUD + combat feedback), then menus, then tooltips. Each area gets thorough treatment.


~2–3 weeks. Each area feels "done" before moving on.

C

Comprehensive v2.0 overhaul

Design and implement everything — every improvement across all 6 areas, including the ambitious ones (cinematic boss spawn, combo system, full tooltip framework). Ship it as one big update.


~3–4 weeks. A real "version 2.0" moment.
